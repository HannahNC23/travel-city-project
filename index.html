Why Nottingham?

Nottingham, the city of caves

Nottingham has the UK's largest network of caves - over 800 are hidden beneath its streets. Carved out of sandstone, the caves that cover the city centre's underground have been variously used over the years as a tannery, public house cellars, and as an air raid shelter.
